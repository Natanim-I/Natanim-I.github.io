Background

I hail from Ethiopia, specifically from the vibrant city of Mizan Teferi, nestled within the lush landscapes and rich cultural tapestry of this East African nation. Ethiopia, often referred to as the "cradle of civilization," boasts a history that stretches back millennia, with its roots deeply entrenched in ancient traditions and a heritage that encompasses diverse ethnicities, languages, and religions. Renowned for its stunning landscapes, including the breathtaking Simien Mountains and the Great Rift Valley, Ethiopia also holds a unique distinction as the only African country never to be colonized, a testament to its resilience and strength throughout history. Its rich cuisine, highlighted by injera (a sourdough flatbread) and aromatic spices, captivates the senses, while its deeply ingrained cultural practices, such as traditional music, dance, and coffee ceremonies, showcase the warmth and hospitality of its people.

Towards the close of 2022, I embarked on a new chapter of my life by journeying to the United States. This transition marked a significant milestone, opening doors to diverse opportunities and experiences. The move from the enchanting landscapes and bustling streets of Mizan Teferi to the dynamic and culturally diverse environment of the United States has been a transformative experience, one filled with the excitement of embracing new perspectives, learning, and contributing to this melting pot of cultures. As I navigate this new terrain, I carry with me the rich heritage and cherished values instilled in me from my Ethiopian upbringing, blending them with the newfound experiences and knowledge gained in this land of boundless possibilities.
